How it works

  1. Connect your wallet using the popup (desktop or mobile wallets supported).
  2. The dashboard reads on-chain activity via Covalent (client-side) and optional data sources.
  3. Your ProofDrop score and badge are calculated using editable rules from config.json.

Replace YOUR_COVALENT_API_KEY in config.json with your Covalent key before public use.
